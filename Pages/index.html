ABOUT US

Ashram Gandhi Puri

Ashram Gandhi Puri is a non-formal educational institution that aims to provide deliberate religious coaching, moving towards a balance between religious theory obtained in formal schools and the practice mandated by the religion itself. At Ashram Gandhi Puri Klungkung, ashram residents are forged through various spiritual sadhana activities such as Puja, Gita chanting, Sarirashrama, Upanishads, and Yoga. Students who study here are also introduced to organic farming and a healthy and simple lifestyle, so they are ready to jump into society later.

Historical Journey

Important Milestones of Ashram Gandhi Puri

1997

Establishment of Shanti Ashram

On September 6, 1997, Ida Rsi Putra Manuaba (Agus Indra Udayana) founded this institution under the name Shanti Ashram, inspired by four extraordinary women in his life. His initial vision was to improve the quality of disadvantaged younger generations through spiritual education and noble values.

2001

Birth of Ashram Gandhi Puri

Coinciding with the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda on January 12, 2001, Shanti Ashram officially changed its name to Ashram Gandhi Puri, marking the birth of the third wing of the Vivekananda Yoga Sanstha Institute. This new name affirms the commitment to carry on the ideals of Mahatma Gandhi.

2011

Jamnalal Bajaj International Award

Ida Rsi Putra Manuaba received the India International Jamnalal Bajaj Award 2011, an international recognition of his dedication in developing human resources and applying Gandhian values in Indonesia.

2020

Padma Shri Award from the Indian Government

The President of India Ram Nath Kovind and PM Narendra Modi awarded the Padma Shri Award to Ida Rsi Putra Manuaba at Rashtrapati Bhavan, for his extraordinary contribution as a social worker with an innovative, creative, inclusive, and collaborative approach.

2022

25th Anniversary Celebration & Vivekananda Bhavan

The Indian Ambassador to Indonesia inaugurated Vivekananda Bhavan at Ashram Gandhi Puri Sevagram Klungkung as a training center for yoga, spa, and Ayurveda. The ashram also sent 25 Shantisena abroad including to Turkey, Dubai, Poland, and India.

2025

28 Years — Uniting Spirituality, Culture & Humanity

The 28th anniversary celebration at Sevagram Klungkung was attended by the Indian Ambassador HE Sandeep Chakravorty. The Statue of Acharya Vinoba Bhave was also inaugurated, a symbol of the fusion of Indian wisdom and Bali-Nusantara local wisdom. The expansion continues with the ground-breaking of Vishvagram Gunaksa.

THE FOUNDER

Ida Rsi Putra Manuaba

Ida Rsi Putra Manuaba is a Hindu cleric, social activist, and founder of Ashram Gandhi Puri in Klungkung, Bali. He is widely known for his dedication in the fields of education, yoga, as well as humanitarian and environmental advocacy.
